Sydney Car Rental Locations

Sydney Car Rental Australia offers convenient car rental services in major tourism destinations across Australia. For those traveling to Queensland, our Cairns location serves the far north region. In South East Queensland, we have branches in Sunshine Coast, Brisbane, and Gold Coast. Our main international locations include Sydney and Melbourne, and we also operate in Adelaide, Perth, Hobart, Launceston, Canberra, and Darwin.

Our city-based branches are strategically located to serve your rental needs, with easy access and convenient pick-up and return options. Our friendly staff are ready to assist you with any questions about your destination or rental requirements.
